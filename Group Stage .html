The 48 teams qualified from different qualification points will fight for their glory from here.

The 48 teams will divided equally into 3 groups of 16, each group exclusively play in a certain week, with each week consists of 4 matchdays. And each matchday consists of 6 matches for a total of 24 matches per week. Top 3 teams of each group will directly advance to Grand Finals, the 4th to 11th place of each group will qualified to Survival Stage. For the bottom 5 teams of each group were eliminated.
