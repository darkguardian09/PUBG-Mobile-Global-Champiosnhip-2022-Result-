PUBG Mobile Global Championship had been won by S2G Esports!

S2G Esports won the PMGC 2022 among the 50 pro teams. They became the first Turkey team to win this glory and they break the record made by Nova Esports!

The 48 teams were invited through different Pro League Championship at Southeast Asia, South Asia, MEA, Americas and Europe, or others league like Peacekeeper Elite League, PEL Qualifier, Pro Series Korea, Japan League and Rivals Cup.

Here are the informations about PMGC 2022.

Organizers : KRAFTON, Level Infinite, VSPN

Sponsorships : Xperia, Predator, Prime Gaming

Official Device : Sony Xperia 1 IV

Game Mode : Squads TPP

Platform : Mobile

Type : Offline

Location : Jakarta and Kuala Lumpur

Venue : Jakarta International Expo (Grand Finals) and Battle Arena (League)

Prize Pool : $4,000,000 USD

Start and End Date : 2022-11-10 until 2023-01-08
